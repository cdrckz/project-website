Traditional Jeepneys: An icon of Filipino Culture

A symbol of Filipino culture and resilience.

Traditional Jeepneys

One of the distinct objects in Filipino culture is the jeepney. This is the most common mode of public transport in the Philippines, adorned with colorful stickers and hand-painted artworks. It is an icon in Pinoy culture. Jeepneys have been used as a mode of transportation in the Philippines since the 1950s. After World War II, American troops left several Willys Jeep in the country, which Filipinos modified and turned into bus-like vehicles that can transport passengers.
