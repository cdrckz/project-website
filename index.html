Traditional Jeepneys: An icon of Filipino Culture

A symbol of Filipino culture and resilience.

Traditional Jeepneys

Traditional jeepneys are a cultural icon of the Philippines, renowned for their vibrant colors and unique design. They have been the primary mode of public transportation for decades, connecting communities across the country.

Beyond transportation, jeepneys represent the resilience and creativity of the Filipino people, as they were originally repurposed from military jeeps after World War II. Their legacy continues to inspire.
